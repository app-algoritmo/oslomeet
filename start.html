Common questions

What people ask before starting

Is it really free to start?

Yes. Creating an account, building your profile, and seeing your matches is completely free. You only pay when you want to contact a match.

How does the AI matching work?

Our algorithm analyzes 50+ signals including industry fit, growth stage, business culture, geographic goals, and strategic objectives to find true compatibility.

What can I see before paying?

You can see the company name, country, sector, compatibility score, and a brief description of each match. Full contact details require a plan.

Is my data safe and GDPR compliant?

Yes. All profiles are verified and data is handled in full compliance with GDPR. Your information is never shared without your explicit consent.

Which countries are on the platform?

Oslo Meet connects companies across Europe, Brazil, China, and the Nordic countries. New regions are added as we grow.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans can be cancelled at any time. Founding Member pricing is locked for life as long as you stay active.
