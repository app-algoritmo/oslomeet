How Our AI Matching Works

No random networking. Our algorithm connects the right companies at the right time, powered by semantic analysis and real business signals.

The Engine

From Profile to Perfect Match

A transparent, 4-step process that turns your business data into high-value connections.

Step 1

Smart Onboarding

You tell us your sector, goals, tech stack, and collaboration needs. Our system structures this into semantic tags and business vectors.

Step 2

AI Embedding & Analysis

Text is converted into high-dimensional embeddings. The AI maps your profile against 50+ compatibility signals in the Oslo/Nordic ecosystem.

Step 3

Match Scoring

A compatibility score (0-100%) is calculated based on strategic alignment, market overlap, tech synergy, and mutual goals.

Step 4

Smart Recommendations

You receive a curated feed of high-potential matches. The AI Concierge can draft personalized introductions or schedule meetings.
