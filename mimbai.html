What is MIMBAI?

MIMBAI stands for Multipurpose Intelligent Modular Business AI — an independent AI platform by Digital Solutions with 7 specialized modules, a project dashboard and AI-generated downloads.

MIMBAI is a standalone AI platform built by Digital Solutions and powered by Anthropic Claude. It delivers specialized intelligence across 7 modules: Project Generator, Excel & Reports, Finance & ROI, HR & People, Presentations, Content Engine and RegTech.

Process

How Oslo Meet Uses MIMBAI

A 4-step pipeline from raw company data to high-confidence business match recommendations.

// 01

Profile Ingestion

Company data — sector, goals, technologies, markets, collaboration preferences — is structured into semantic tags and normalized business vectors.

// 02

AI Analysis

MIMBAI analyzes each company profile using Anthropic Claude, extracting intent, strategic fit and market positioning into structured intelligence.

// 03

Compatibility Scoring

MIMBAI computes a 0–100% compatibility score using weighted signals: industry fit, strategic goals, tech synergy, market overlap, and collaboration intent.

// 04

Match Delivery

Top matches surface in each company's Oslo Meet dashboard. MIMBAI can draft personalized introductions based on match context and shared goals.
