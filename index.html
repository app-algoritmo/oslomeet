About Oslo Meet

Oslo Meet is a comprehensive Directory of Ideas & Businesses that serves as a bridge between innovative entrepreneurs, forward-thinking investors, and conscious consumers worldwide.

We create a dynamic ecosystem where startups, small businesses, and social projects can showcase their innovations, connect with like-minded individuals, and find the resources they need to thrive.

Through podcasts, interviews, and networking events, we amplify authentic voices and foster meaningful connections that drive real business growth and social impact.

Our Mission

To create the world's most connected entrepreneurial ecosystem where innovative ideas meet opportunities, experiences inspire solutions, and businesses grow through authentic relationships.
